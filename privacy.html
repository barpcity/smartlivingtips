Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Smart Living Tips ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our content management platform and services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you connect your social media accounts via OAuth, we receive access tokens to manage content on your behalf. We do not store your social media passwords.
  • Content Data: Content you create, schedule, or publish through our platform, including titles, descriptions, images, and scheduling preferences.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including features used and performance metrics.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our content management services
  • To schedule and publish content on your connected social media accounts
  • To generate AI-powered content suggestions and optimizations
  • To improve our platform and user experience

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption. We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission.

5. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with third-party services including Pinterest API and Google Gemini AI. Each service has its own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them. We only share the minimum data necessary for these integrations to function.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. Upon account deletion, we remove all stored tokens and personal data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to connected social media accounts at any time
  • Opt out of any analytics or tracking

8. Cookies

Our platform uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us
