What The Platform Does

The app is used by the Smart Living Tips owner to manage owned Pinterest content. It does not scrape Pinterest, does not mass-save third-party Pins, and does not publish deceptive or unrelated links.

Original Pin Creation

Creates vertical images, titles, descriptions, alt text, and board assignments for owned editorial guides.

User-Approved Scheduling

Queues content with conservative daily limits so publishing is intentional, reviewable, and tied to owned boards.

Pinterest API Integration

Uses OAuth to connect the business account, read boards, create Pins, and sync performance data.
